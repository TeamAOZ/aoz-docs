Turn the connected Bot to the left or the right.
## Parameters

- **direction&dollar;**: "left" or "right"
- **speed**: The speed at which to move Bot. A positive value indicate a move forward, and a negative a move backward, ranging from -200 to 200.
- **duration**: The duration of the movement, in seconds, ranging from 0 to ﹢∞, in seconds. When "null", it indicates that Bot keeps moving at the specified speed until it receives a command instructing it to stop moving.
