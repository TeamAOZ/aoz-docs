Makes the colors of the LEDs on the LED strip connected to the specified port roll from left to right by the specified number of positions.
## Parameters

- **step**: Number of positions by which the colors of the LEDs roll; setting range
- **step**: Range of the LED color rolling; setting range
- **port**: The number of the port where the strip is connected, 1, 2, or 0 for all ports
