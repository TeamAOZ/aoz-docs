## Syntax

**Filled Arc** *x*, *y*, *radius1*, *angle1*, *angle2*, *rotation*, *counterclockwise*

## Parameters


- **x**: The horizontal coordinate of the centre of the arc
- **y**: The vertical coordinate of the centre of the arc
- **radius1**: The radius of the arc
- **angle1**: The angle from which the arc is drawn from. 0 degrees is right-horizonal from the centre.
- **angle2**: The angle to which the arc will be drawn to
- **rotation**: Optional. Angle of rotation of the arc
- **counterclockwise**: Optional. Direction the arc will be drawn from angle1

## Description
Draws an arc of a circle between 2 angles and joins the ends with a straight line and fills the shape using the current ink, border and fill settings in the current screen. 

## Example Code

This example draws 100 random Filled Arcs in various random colours.

```basic
ink 2
for f=1 to 100
	r=rnd(100 to 200)
	colour 2,rgb(rnd(80 to 255), rnd(80 to 255), rnd(80 to 255))
	r=rnd(100 to 200)
	filled arc rnd(200 to 1720),rnd(200 to 880),r,rnd(0 to 160),rnd(200 to 360),rnd(360)
	wait 0.5
next f
```
