Sets the angles of the servos connected to ports 1 to 4 at the same time
## Parameters

- **angle1**: Angle of the servo connected to port S1; setting range
- **angle2**: Angle of the servo connected to port S2; setting range
- **angle3**: Angle of the servo connected to port S3; setting range
- **angle4**: Angle of the servo connected to port S4; setting range
