Play a video loaded with the "Load Asset" command
## Parameters

- **video**: Index of video.

<BR>
Example with the video in the ressources/assets folder:

Load Asset "yourvideo.mp4","intro" :  Actor "video", X=1020, Y=20, video$="intro"
