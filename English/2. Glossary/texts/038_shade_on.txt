Switch on Shade setting for printing in the current text window.
## Parameters

- **intensity**: Optional. Percentage value to apply to the colour to brighten or darken it. To darken the colour, the value should be between 0 and 100. To brighten it, above 100. Be aware that if you over-brighten a colour.
