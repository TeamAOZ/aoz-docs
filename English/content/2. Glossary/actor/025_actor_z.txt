# Actor Z
<!--

@description:

Returns the Z position of an actor defined by the `Z` property of the `Actor` command.

## Syntax
>A =  **Actor Z**( actor_index )

## Parameters
|**Parameter**|**Type**|**Description**|
|---|---|---|
|**`Index`**|index|Index of the Actor.|
