Rotate a screen around it|;s hot-spot on display. Warning, on software renderers this instruction will slow down the application
## Parameters

- _index_ (optional): The index of the screen, if ommited the instruction will affect the current screen.
- **angle**: The angle of the rotation of the screen. Default in radian, and degrees after the "Degree" instruction has been used
